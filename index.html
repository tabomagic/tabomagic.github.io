Key Features

Consolidated tab view

Whenever you open a new tab page, you'll see all your open tabs, cleanly laid out for you.

Clean up tabs instantly

Quickly close tabs you don't need with a single click or just select and hit 'delete'.

Never hunt for a tab again

Quickly find the exact tab you're looking for with the built-in search function.

Organize tabs how you want

You can see your tabs laid out in one of four different ways: by the site, by the tab group, by the last access time, or by the window.

Get back to any page from your history or bookmarks

The built-in search function also finds matches in your browser bookmarks and history, so you can always get back to the page you need.

Instantly create new tab groups

Just drag the card for one tab onto another. Any you can rename the group by clicking on the group name.
